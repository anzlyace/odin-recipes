Tortang Talong

Description

This incrdeible egg...plant dish uses eggs and eggplant together to make one of my favourite Filipino foods, tortang talong! This was one of my moms favourites and she would make these alomost every week. Such a memory dish!

Ingredients

  • Eggplants that can fit into your airfryer
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-3 eggs beaten
  • Cornstarch(optional)
  • Fish Sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Soy sauce, tomatoes, green onions for garnish
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 3-4 garlic
  • 1 tomato diced

Steps

  1. Puncture eggplants with a knife to prevent bursting in air fryer. Coat in oil and air fry for 30 mins at 400 degrees turning once or bake in oven 350Â° for 1 hour. Meanwhile cool garlic, onions, pork and tomato till cooked.
  2. Allow eggplant to cool and gently peel skin off of eggplant. Season with salt and pepper. Flatten eggplant gently with the back of a fork.
  3. Dip eggplant into eggs. I add a tad of fish sauce to the eggs. Dredge in cornstarch which is also optional.
  4. Fry in 2 tbsp oil over medium heat. Cook 3-4 minutes per side. I spoon pork mixture onto eggplant as it cooks. Serve with a side of garlic rice and your choice of toppings.
