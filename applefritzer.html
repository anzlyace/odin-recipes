Apple Fritzer

Description

My famous 🍏 Fritzer recipe! Named after my grandfather. And me. This is the perfect treat to make at home, or to bring to the potluck!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 c diced green apples
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 1/4 c flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • pinch nutmeg and ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 c milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place 🍎🍏’s in bowl with lemon juice. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cooled melted butter.
  2. In another bowl, combine all dry ingredients. Add dry ingredients to wet and mix well until a loose dough is formed.
  3. Fold in the 🍏🍎 Fill a frying pan with about 3-4 cups veg oil. Heat oil over medium heat. Drop a scoop of batter into the oil. Fry 4-5 minutes total flipping half way.
